⬆ Drag this button to your bookmarks bar
(or right-click → Add to Bookmarks)

How to Use
1
Open Discord in your browser and navigate to the #speak-swedish-with-katrin channel
2
Scroll to the voice message you want to download
3
Click the 🎙️ Download Discord Audio bookmark in your bar — a panel will appear listing all voice messages found on the page
4
Click Download next to the message you want — the .ogg file saves to your Downloads folder
5
Drop the file into Svenska Studio and transcribe!
Notes & Troubleshooting

💡 Safari users: Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible — View → Show Favorites Bar (or ⇧⌘B)

💡 No messages found? Discord loads messages dynamically. Scroll through the channel first so the voice messages are visible on screen, then click the bookmarklet.

💡 Download blocked? Safari may ask to allow downloads from discord.com — click Allow. This is a one-time prompt.

💡 Links expired? Discord CDN links expire after a few hours. If you see an error, reload the Discord page and run the bookmarklet again.
